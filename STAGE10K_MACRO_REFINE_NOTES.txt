Stage 10K — Macro system refinement

Goal:
- make Macro 1 / Macro 2 roles clearer before deeper transport coupling

Macro semantics clarified:
- P5 / FILTER_MACRO -> breath_amount
  * amount of autonomous movement
- P6 / MORPH -> harmonic_blend
  * dry harmonic -> hybrid -> destructive

What changed:
- harmonic_blend is now interpreted through 3 internal zones:
  * blend_dry_harmonic
  * blend_hybrid
  * blend_destructive
- harmonic support is stronger in the lower region of Macro 2
- FM/destructive influence is concentrated more in the upper region
- stutter modulation was reduced again to keep behavior readable
- note fold modulation was slightly tamed for clarity

Result:
- Macro 2 should now feel less ambiguous:
  * low = cleaner / more harmonic
  * mid = hybrid
  * high = more destructive / FM-driven
- Macro 1 remains the global "life amount" control
