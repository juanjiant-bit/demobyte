Stage 3C — Float low-end and controllable depth

Applied:
- Added low_depth_macro_ and float_body_macro_ in AudioEngine.
- Mapped them at block-rate from macro/tonal context and routed low_depth to DrumEngine.
- Kick now has a controllable sub layer tied to low_depth.
- NoteVoice and SnapshotVoice algorithms now add a low-frequency float/operator body layer with per-algorithm weight.
- Subgrave is no longer effectively fixed:
  * idle floor depends on low_depth
  * active peak depends on low_depth
  * kick ducks sub harder as low_depth increases
  * final sub mix depends on low_depth and macro motion density
- Final master mix now adds sub as its own bus instead of injecting it directly into voice_m.

Intent:
- Depth should not depend only on a constant sub oscillator.
- Low-end should increasingly come from the float/operator layer itself.
- low_depth controls how much foundation is present.
- float_body controls how much low-end is born inside the voice algorithms.

Limitations:
- This pass does not yet add true floatbeat formula evaluation.
- It is a bridge stage: more low-end resolution from the operator/float side, with controlled sub support.
