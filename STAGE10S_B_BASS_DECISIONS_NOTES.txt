Stage 10S-B — Route audio decisions into bass behavior

Goal:
- make the new decision layer affect musical behavior, starting with the bass engine

Added:
- bass_hold_steps_
- bass_force_advance_

Decision routing:
- energy_peak:
  * briefly repeats/holds the previous bass step
  * reinforces the phrase
- energy_drop:
  * nudges the bass to advance/release the phrase
- kick_like:
  * can encourage octave jumps
  * slightly boosts bass gate energy

Important:
- this is still conservative
- it does not replace the existing bass pattern system
- it only biases phrase behavior using the detected audio events

Intent:
- move from "bass is modulated" to "bass makes simple musical decisions"
- keep the behavior bounded before routing decisions into NoteVoice or FX
