Stage 5E — Floatbeat Dry Control

Added:
- floatbeat_dry_macro_ in AudioEngine
- block-rate target derived from performance macro when perf_macro_ enters the upper zone
- tonal weighting so dry floatbeat is more available on more tonal patches

Behavior:
- low / medium macro: hybrid behavior stays mostly unchanged
- high macro: progressively shifts note_voice and snapshot_voice toward floatbeat dominance
- note_voice gets a stronger dry shift than snapshot_voice

Mix logic:
- bytebeat / operator / float_voice weights are attenuated as dry_amt rises
- floatbeat weight is boosted
- final weights are renormalized to keep gain stable

Intent:
- make floatbeat audibly expose itself without hard-muting the other layers
- keep the instrument performative rather than turning dry floatbeat into a technical toggle
