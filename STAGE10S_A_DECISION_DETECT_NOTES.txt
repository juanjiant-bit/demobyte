Stage 10S-A — Audio decision detection only

Goal:
- add the first explicit decision layer derived from the generated audio
- keep this pass zero-risk: detect only, do not route decisions into sound yet

Added:
- AudioDecisionState in AudioEngine:
  * kick_like
  * snare_like
  * energy_peak
  * energy_drop
  * prev_env
  * small hold counters to avoid chattering
- update_audio_decision_state()

Inputs used:
- breath_env_mix_
- breath_spike_mix_
- breath_analysis_.low_proxy
- breath_analysis_.high_proxy

Important:
- This stage does NOT change sound behavior yet.
- It only computes stable, lightweight decision events so the next stage can route them into:
  * bass movement
  * note articulation
  * gate/stutter decisions

Intent:
- move from continuous modulation only toward audio-driven musical decisions
- do it without introducing new behavioral bugs in the same pass
