Optimization audit pass H

Applied:
- LeadOsc hot-path optimization:
  * feed_bytebeat() now slews the modulator in Q15 instead of float.
  * set_mod_depth() caches FM/AM amounts as fixed-point (Q12/Q14).
  * process() now applies FM/AM mostly with integer math.
  * portamento slews delta directly instead of recomputing freq->delta with floats each sample.
- AudioEngine voice/lead caching:
  * NoteVoice caches base_freq_hz on NOTE_ON.
  * refresh_control_block() caches root_freq_cached_ once per block.
  * note voice operator layer, snapshot root voice and sub now reuse cached frequencies.

Why this pass:
- Lead oscillator and note/root quantization were still part of the always-on hot path.
- These changes reduce float and repeated Quantizer calls in Core0 while preserving the current musical architecture.
