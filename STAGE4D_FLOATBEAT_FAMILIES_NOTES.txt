Stage 4D — Floatbeat families

Applied:
- Replaced the single floatbeat seed with a family-based floatbeat layer.
- Added floatbeat_algo(..., algo) with 4 variants aligned to the current operator families:
  0 = deep sine stack
  1 = metallic / bright hybrid
  2 = sub bass focused
  3 = noisy resonant-ish texture
- Moved floatbeat_seed.h to fast_trig-based helpers to avoid direct sinf() calls.
- note_voice and snapshot_voice now pick their floatbeat family from op_algo.

Intent:
- Give the float layer real character variation instead of one shared tone.
- Keep the architecture compatible with the current voice routing.
- Move further toward a true hybrid bytebeat + operator + floatbeat instrument.
