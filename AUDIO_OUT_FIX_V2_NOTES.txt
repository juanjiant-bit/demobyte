Audio out fix v2

Changes:
- Replaced ad-hoc 16+16 packed stream with standard Philips I2S timing.
- Now uses 32-bit slots per channel (64 BCLK per stereo frame).
- audio.write() pushes one 32-bit word for Left and one 32-bit word for Right.
- 16-bit PCM is left-aligned inside each 32-bit slot, which matches PCM5102A I2S mode.

Hardware reminders:
- PCM5102A FMT must be LOW / not strapped for left-justified.
- XSMT must be HIGH to avoid mute on boards that expose that strap.
