
Stage 10I — Bass sequence sync

What changed:
- Replaced the previous free-running/internal bass clock with a sequencer-like musical clock derived from BPM.
- Added persistent bass engine state to AudioEngine:
  * bass_seq_step_
  * bass_pattern_id_
  * bass_seq_accum_
- Pattern changes now happen more slowly and predictably.
- Bass octave jumps remain available, but are tied to stronger breath + rhythm conditions.
- Bass transposition now handles octave offsets above 12 semitones safely.

Important:
- This is not yet transport-locked to sequencer start/stop events.
- It is, however, musically synced to the current BPM and much closer to the sequencer feel.
- This pass is intended as a safer and more coherent timing foundation before full transport coupling.
