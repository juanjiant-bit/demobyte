Stage 9A — LP Filter

Added:
- dsp/lp_filter.h: simple one-pole stereo low-pass filter
- DspChain support:
  * FX_LP bit
  * lp_.init/reset/process
  * set_lp_cutoff(...)
  * active-mask integration

Routing:
- LP sits after HP in the DSP chain.
- audio_engine drives LP cutoff from float_plane_bias_ and macro_motion_.density_s:
  * more float / more density => lower cutoff
  * cleaner / calmer states => brighter output

Intent:
- soften harsh top-end
- make complex/floating patches more synth-like
- provide a low-risk, audible improvement before hardware testing
