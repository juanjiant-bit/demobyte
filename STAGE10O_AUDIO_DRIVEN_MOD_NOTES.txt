Stage 10O — Audio-driven modulation core

Goal:
- begin using the actual generated audio as a modulation source
- keep the analysis extremely lightweight and RP2040-safe

Added:
- BreathAnalysisState in AudioEngine:
  * env
  * spike
  * low_proxy
  * high_proxy
  * lightweight block accumulators
- update_breath_analysis(float mono_abs)
- finalize_breath_analysis_block()

How it works:
- recent output amplitude and edge/transient change are accumulated
- block-rate smoothing derives:
  * sustained energy (env / low_proxy)
  * transient/edge activity (spike / high_proxy)

Applied conservatively to:
- note_fm_byte
- note_decay
- bass_gate
- drum_decay
- stutter_amount
- harmonic_support

Intent:
- move toward the "macro respiratorio autogenerado" idea
- let the instrument react to its own audio without needing heavy FFT or pitch tracking
- keep this first pass bounded and debuggable before wider routing
