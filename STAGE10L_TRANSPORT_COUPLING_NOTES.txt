
Stage 10L — Bass transport coupling

Goal:
- move the bass engine closer to real sequencer transport behavior

What changed:
- update_bass_movement() now prefers sequencer-coupled stepping via:
  * state_mgr_->get_current_step_index()
  * state_mgr_->is_transport_running()
- when transport-coupled stepping is unavailable in a moment, it falls back to the prior BPM-derived accumulator
- pattern changes still happen only on phrase boundaries
- bass envelope is slightly calmer when transport is not running

Important integration note:
- This stage assumes StateManager exposes:
  * get_current_step_index()
  * is_transport_running()
- If those helpers do not yet exist in your current tree, they should be added as thin accessors to the existing sequencer/transport state.
- The rest of the bass logic remains conservative and compatible with previous stages.
