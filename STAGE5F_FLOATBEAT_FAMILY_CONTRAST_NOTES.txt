Stage 5F — Floatbeat family contrast

Applied:
- Increased contrast between floatbeat families in audio/floatbeat_seed.h
  * deep: more stable and deeper low-end
  * metallic: brighter and harsher when exposed
  * sub: heavier sub emphasis
  * noisy: more wobble and texture
- Added per-algorithm dry_amt scaling in audio_engine.cpp
  * deep/sub move harder toward dry floatbeat
  * metallic resists dry floatbeat more
  * noisy stays semi-hybrid

Intent:
- make floatbeat dry mode reveal clearly different identities
- avoid all algorithms collapsing toward the same "float" character
- keep the instrument expressive and patch-dependent in the upper macro range
