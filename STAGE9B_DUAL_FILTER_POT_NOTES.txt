Stage 9B — Dual filter on one pot

Implemented the filter the way requested:
- 0..50% of the pot => LP region
- 50..100% of the pot => HP region
- dry/blend zone around the middle

Technical behavior:
- Added DspChain::set_dual_filter_control(float norm)
- LP and HP now have independent wet mixes (lp_mix_ / hp_mix_)
- Around the center:
  * LP fades out toward dry from 45% to 55%
  * HP fades in after 55%
- audio_engine now drives the filter from one normalized control path:
  cached_ctx_.tonal

Result:
- one pot acts like a dual analog-style tone control
- center stays usable/dry
- left side softens with LP
- right side thins/brightens with HP
